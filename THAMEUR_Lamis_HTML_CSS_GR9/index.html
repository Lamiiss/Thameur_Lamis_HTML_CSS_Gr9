Bonnie

Bonnie the Bunny or, often just Bonnie is the secondary antagonist of Five Nights at Freddy's and Five Nights at Freddy's 2. Like his fellow animatronics Freddy, Chica (and formerly Foxy), Bonnie is a friendly child entertainer during the day housed at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, but stuffs any human he sees during the night into a suit.
